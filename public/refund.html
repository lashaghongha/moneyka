Refund Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

MoneyKa subscriptions are processed by Paddle.com, who acts as Merchant of Record. Paddle handles all refund requests in accordance with their policies.

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

Within 30 days of purchase: You are eligible for a full refund on your subscription if you are not satisfied with the service, no questions asked.

After 30 days: Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your situation.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at support@moneyka.app with your registered phone number and the reason for the refund. We will process your request within 3–5 business days.

Alternatively, you can request a refund directly through Paddle's buyer support portal.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing immediately. You will retain access to your paid plan features until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused days after the 30-day window.

4. Free Plan

The free plan requires no payment and is not subject to this refund policy.

5. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions: support@moneyka.app
