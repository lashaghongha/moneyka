Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use MoneyKa:

  • Name — provided during registration
  • Phone number — used for account identification and recovery
  • Device ID — a randomly generated identifier for your device
  • Subscription plan — free, Pro, or Elite
  • Usage timestamps — first seen and last seen dates

Financial data (transactions, goals, budgets) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To allow account recovery across devices using phone number and password
  • To sync your subscription plan (e.g. if updated by admin)
  • To send subscription reminder push notifications (if enabled)

3. Data Storage

Account data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway.app (US-based). Financial transaction data remains on your device's local storage and is never uploaded.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Paddle — payment processing. Paddle may collect billing information. See Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI / AI providers — anonymized spending summaries may be sent to generate AI advice. No personal identifiers are included.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@moneyka.app.

7. Security

Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 before storage. PINs are hashed locally on your device and never transmitted. All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.

8. Children's Privacy

MoneyKa is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions: support@moneyka.app
